About Us

Welcome to NATGO the app that helps you plan out your most relaxing vacations or adventurous getaways exploring the world’s most magnificent national parks! We're dedicated to giving you the best and easiest experience in finding which park to explore, what activities and amenities to enjoy and where your next adventure will take place!

Founded in 2022, NATGO has come a long way from its inception. Starting out as a web app to list all national parks in the US, as time went on it evolved into what we offer today, a full fledge service of planning your next trip!

Services

Here at NATGO we provide numerous services, such as locating which national park you’d like to explore next! As well as being able to look for hotels, restaurants, and activities in the area while you embark on your next adventure!
